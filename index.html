Efficient step-by-step supply flow

Our Export Execution Process

Step 01

Inquiry Received

Buyer submits request detailing grades, target pricing, quantities, packaging specs, and destination port (CIF/FOB).

Step 02

Quotation Submission

Sanover sends formal Proforma Invoice and commercial pricing proposals matching dynamic market rates.

Step 03

Sample Approval

We dispatch physical air-freight samples for lab testing and buyer sensory evaluation prior to contract sealing.

Step 04

Contract Execution

Signing of export agreement and setting up payment methods (LC at sight / advanced wire transfer).

Step 05

Production & Processing

Sourcing raw batches, cleaning, milling, Sortex grading, and packaging in our ISO certified facilities.

Step 06

Third-Party Inspection

Independent surveyors (SGS/Geo-Chem) extract random samples to verify weight, grading, and moisture benchmarks.

Step 07

Documentation

Compilation of Bill of Lading, Phytosanitary, fumigation certificates, certificate of origin, and custom clear paperworks.

Step 08

Port Shipment

Loading cargo containers onto vessels at maritime shipping ports (Kandla / Mundra / JNPT) for transit.

Step 09

Delivery & Tracking

Vessel reaches destination. Buyer clears customs using dispatched original document sets and takes cargo custody.
